Carbonara

Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 140g Pancetta or Guanciale
  • 1 large Eggs
  • 3 eggs yolks
  • 75g grated pecorino romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Directions

Step 1 - Cook the Pasta

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.

    Stir in spaghetti and return to a boil.

    Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.

    Reserve a little pasta water, then drain.

Step 2

  1. Meanwhile, heat a skillet over medium heat; add guanciale, cook and stir until crisp, 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 3

  1. Whisk egg yolks and whole egg together in a bowl; whisk in 75g grated Pecorino Romano cheese, salt, and pepper until combined.

Step 4

  1. Stir in guanciale. Add spaghetti; toss until evenly coated. Season to taste with extra cheese, salt, and pepper; serve.
