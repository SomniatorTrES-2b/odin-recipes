Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 450g Beef
  • 900g of Spaghetti sauce
  • 900g cottage Cheeses
  • 300g Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of Parsley
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 9 Lasaga sheets
  • 120ml Water

    • How to make lasagna

    Steps :

    1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
    2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
    3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
    4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
    5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.

      6. Directions

      Step 1 - Preheat the Oven
      1. Gather all ingredients and Preheat your oven to 175°C

      Step 2- Cook the Meat Sauce

      1. In a large frying pan, cook 450 g ground beef over medium-high heat until browned and crumbly (about 8–10 minutes).
      2. Drain excess fat, then stir in 900 g spaghetti sauce.
      3. Simmer for 5 minutes.

      Step 3 - Prepare the Cheese Mixture

      In a large mixing bowl, combine:

      1. 900 g cottage cheese
      2. 200 g of the shredded mozzarella (reserve 100 g for topping)
      3. 2 eggs
      4. 25 g of the grated Parmesan (reserve the rest for topping)
      5. 2 tablespoons dried parsley
      6. Salt and pepper
      7. Mix it until its well blended

      Step 4- Assenble de lasagna

      In baking dish.

      1. Spread about 180 ml of the meat sauce on the bottom.
      2. Layer 3 lasagna sheets over the sauce.
      3. Spread about 450 g of the cheese mixture on top.
      4. Add 60 ml of meat sauce over the cheese layer.
      5. Repeat this layer one more time.
      6. Top with the final 3 lasagna sheets, then the remaining sauce, the reserved 100 g mozzarella, and the remaining 25 g Parmesan.
      7. Carefully pour 120 ml water along the inner edges of the dish.
      8. Cover with aluminium foil

      Step 5- Bake

      1. Bake covered at 175°C for 45 minutes.
      2. hen remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes to brown the top.

      Step 6- Rest and Serve

      1. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
      2. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
