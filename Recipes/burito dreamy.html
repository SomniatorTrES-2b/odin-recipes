Dreamy Burrito

Hola amigos, Burrito dreamy its my special meal if you don't have time and it's good (nothing fancy)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of Mustard
  • White ham or Meat

How to make a Dreamy Burrito

Steps:

    Step 1 :

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and add salt and pepper.

    2. Step 2 :

  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour in the beaten eggs. Cook until scrambled.

    3. Step 3 :

  3. Chop the tomato and add it to the scrambled eggs.

    4. Step 4 :

  4. Place the tortilla on a plate, spread mustard on it, and layer with white ham or meat.

    5. Step 5 :

  5. Add the scrambled egg and tomato mixture on top of the meat.

    6. Step 6 :

  6. Wrap the tortilla tightly to form a burrito.

    7. Step 7 :

  7. Eat your Dreamy Burrito!
